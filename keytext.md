## Description
CatKey is the only iOS keyboard dedicated to Catalan language support. When Apple removed Catalan spell checking in iOS 17.4, millions of Catalan speakers lost a basic tool. CatKey brings it back - and makes it better.

BUILT FOR CATALAN SPEAKERS
CatKey was created because no existing keyboard treats Catalan as a first-class language. Gboard and SwiftKey support 75+ languages but none offer dedicated Catalan spell checking. CatKey is Catalan-first, not an afterthought.

CORE FEATURES (FREE FOREVER)
- Catalan spell checking with a 30,000-word dictionary
- Autocorrect that understands Catalan morphology
- Word prediction with smart suggestions
- Seamless Catalan-Spanish bilingual switching with automatic language detection
- Full QWERTY layout with all Catalan characters (a, e, i, o, u with accents, cedilla c, interpunct)
- Long-press access to accented characters
- Haptic and sound feedback

CATALAN PRO - ONE-TIME PURCHASE
Upgrade once, own it forever. No subscriptions.
- Complete 50,000+ word dictionary
- Advanced autocorrect with deeper error detection
- Enhanced word prediction with 5 candidates
- Dialect support: Balearic and Valencian
- Professional dictionaries: legal, medical, education
- Custom dictionary: add your own words
- Theme customization with Catalan-inspired designs
- Keyboard height adjustment
- Priority support

PRIVACY FIRST
CatKey works entirely offline. No data is collected, transmitted, or stored on any server. Your words stay on your device. Full Access is required only for the keyboard to function - it never sends your typing anywhere.

IN-APP PURCHASE
CatKey Pro is a one-time purchase of $3.99. No recurring charges. Restore purchases is supported for reinstallations.
